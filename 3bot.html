WHAT 3BOT MEANS FOR YOU



private data
Your 3Bot gives you full control over your data, having unlimited access to private storage (only you can access), paying only what you use - on the edge. 


communication
Social media, messaging, video calls, emails - all in one place. Your 3Bot is equipped with all the communication tools to interact with your circles. 


digital wallet
Escape the centralised exchanges. Your 3Bot is equipped of its own digital wallet, giving you access to a world of sustainable digital currencies


personal assistant
Need a cab, a hotel room, or a flight? Your 3Bot will be equipped with voice interaction, scheduling, and bot-to-bot communication. 3Bot makes your life simpler.



coworking and education
Our people and children need the right tools to learn and create. 3Bot provides you with the best sources of information anywhere, even in refugee camps. 


personal publisher
3Bot is your personal publisher. Any website, blog or ebook can be published by anyone and shared with the world. A revolution for people and organisations. 

all-in-one access

Our digital lives are scattered across a range of applications and services. Don't you think it's weird to be exposed to countless apps on our devices every day – apps that work in silos, complicating our lives, rather than in conjunction? 3Bot wll become everything you need, all in one place.

Scroll to learn more.

communication tools

Messaging. Email. Video conferencing. Disjointed communication applications. The tools you know and use on a daily basis – but with 3Bot, you're one login away as they all exist in the same location, simplifying your experience. With 3Bot, communication is peer-to-peer, the way it happens in our natural experiences.

information

Knowledge is power. 3Bot will give you access to complementary education tools and services, created to provide people with verifiable and purposeful information. Say goodbye to fake news and articles. Enter a conscious digital world that wants the best information for its people.

complementary banking

Access a digital economy where direct and fair value exchange is enabled and ensured. Tokens are supported by real value – Internet capacity, coffee, an hour of work, or a square meter of rainforest land (for preservation).  And people have access to secured personal wallets to store and exchange instantaneously.

virtual browser

Say goodbye to surfing the web with antivirus, firewall, or VPNs – you don't need these anymore with 3Bot. Just open your private browser and you're two clicks away from all the information in the world, no matter where you are in the world. A browser that does not discriminate you by region.

file manager

Our digital lives are scattered across a range of applications and services with varying levels of security and data privacy. No need for this anymore. 3Bot finds ways to store your files efficiently on the edge, meaning more security, speed, and sustainability, at a lower cost for you.

imagine...

Any application or service you can think of can exist within 3Bot. An extensive, integrated SDK means that 3Bot can grow with the help of developers. We invite you to participate & expand the possibilities of 3Bot. So, let's dream & create solutions to improve the lives of people around the world.
